About Staffona

Egypt's HR Partner for
International Business

We're a specialist HR consultancy based in Alexandria — built to make hiring, paying, and managing people in Egypt simple for any company, anywhere in the world.

Our Mission

Remove Every Barrier to
Hiring in Egypt

Egypt has a deep talent pool — skilled engineers, multilingual professionals, finance and HR specialists — available at competitive rates. But the barriers to hiring legally are real: Arabic contracts, NOSI registration, quarterly tax returns, Labor Office filings.

Staffona exists to remove those barriers entirely. We handle every legal, administrative, and compliance obligation so companies can focus on getting work done — not on navigating Egyptian bureaucracy.

We serve Egyptian SMEs who want to professionalise their HR, and international companies — from the Gulf, UK, US, and Europe — who want to build or manage Egyptian teams without establishing a local entity.

FAQ

Common Questions About Staffona

+Is Staffona a licensed company in Egypt?

Yes. Staffona is a legally registered Egyptian consultancy operating under Egyptian commercial law, with full authority to provide HR outsourcing, payroll administration, and recruitment services.

+What languages does Staffona operate in?

Staffona operates bilingually in Arabic and English. Client communications, contracts, and reports are available in both languages. Our team is fluent in both, enabling direct interaction with Egyptian government authorities and international clients simultaneously.

+How is Staffona different from a general accounting firm?

Staffona is a specialist HR consultancy — not a general accountancy or law firm. Our expertise is deep and narrow: Egyptian employment law, payroll, social insurance, and talent acquisition. This specialisation means faster, more accurate service than a generalist firm that handles HR as one of many practice areas.

+Does Staffona work with startups and small teams?

Yes — and this is actually where we add the most value. A startup hiring its first Egyptian employee faces the same compliance requirements as a large corporation. Staffona makes those requirements manageable at any scale, from 1 to 100+ employees.

+Why should I outsource HR instead of hiring in-house?

In-house HR in Egypt requires hiring a specialist who understands payroll calculations, social insurance filings, Labor Office registrations, and Arabic employment law — and keeping that person continuously updated as regulations change. Staffona gives you that expertise on-demand, at a fraction of the cost, with no risk of staff turnover disrupting compliance. For most companies under 50 employees, outsourced HR is simply cheaper, faster, and safer.

+Why Staffona specifically — what makes you different?

Three things: specialisation, location, and accountability. We focus exclusively on Egypt HR — no side practices, no general consulting. We are based in Alexandria with direct access to government authorities. And we assign a named contact to every client, not a call centre. If something needs fixing, you know exactly who to call and they know your account.
