Managed Employment · EOR Egypt

Hire in Egypt Without
Opening a Local Company

Our managed employment service legally employs your Egyptian team on your behalf — handling all payroll, social insurance, contracts, and compliance. You stay in control. We handle everything else.

How It Works

You Manage the Work.
We Manage the Law.

Staffona becomes the legal employer of your Egyptian team members — signing their contracts, running payroll, registering them with NOSI, and filing taxes. You direct their work as normal.

1
You find (or we recruit) the candidate
You select who you want to hire. We handle the rest from contract to first day.
2
Staffona signs the employment contract
Arabic-language contract compliant with Labor Law 12/2003. Registered with the Labor Office.
3
Monthly payroll & compliance run
We calculate, withhold, and remit salary tax and social insurance every month. You receive one invoice.
4
Exit anytime with 30 days notice
No liquidation process, no long-term commitment. Exit cleanly with one month's notice.
