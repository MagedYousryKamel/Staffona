The unified social insurance law replacing Laws 79/1975 and 108/1976. Covers pensions, disability insurance, occupational injuries, death benefits, and health insurance. New contribution rates phased in over 7 years from 2020.

üìÖ Effective July 2020 üîÑ 7-year phase-in ‚öñÔ∏è Mandatory for all employees
