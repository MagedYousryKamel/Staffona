Methodology

How the Calculator Works

📊

Social Insurance

Applied on insurable wage capped at EGP 16,700/mo. Employee pays 11% (pension 9% + health 1% + EOS 1%). Employer pays 18.75% on top.

💰

Income Tax

Progressive brackets 0%→27.5% per Egyptian Tax Law 2026, with high-income adjustments above EGP 600k/year. Taxable income = annual gross minus SI deductions minus personal exemption (EGP 20,000/yr) minus employer medical premiums (up to EGP 10,000/yr).

🔄

Net ↔ Gross

Switch modes to calculate from desired net take-home. Finds the exact gross needed to deliver your target net using iterative binary search — accurate to EGP 0.05.
