Methodology

How the Calculator Works

📊

Social Insurance

Calculated on the insurable wage (capped at EGP 16,700/mo). Employee pays 11% total (pension 9% + health 1% + EOS 1%). Employer pays 18.75% total.

💰

Income Tax

Progressive brackets from 0% to 27.5% per Egyptian Income Tax Law. Annual taxable income = gross annual minus SI deductions minus personal exemption (EGP 20,000).

🏛️

Mandatory Funds

Martyrs & Victims Fund (0.05% employee). Emergency Relief Fund and Training Fund apply to companies with 30+ employees.
