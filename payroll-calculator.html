Methodology

How the Calculator Works

📊

Social Insurance

Applied on insurable wage capped at EGP 16,700/mo. Employee pays 11% total (pension 9% + health 1% + EOS 1%). Employer pays 18.75%.

💰

Income Tax

Progressive brackets 0%→27.5%. Taxable income = annual gross minus SI deductions minus personal exemption (EGP 20,000/yr standard).

🔄

Net ↔ Gross

Enter a desired take-home salary and the calculator finds the exact gross needed to deliver it, accounting for all deductions.
