Methodology

How the Calculator Works

📊

Social Insurance

Applied on insurable wage capped at EGP 16,700/mo. Employee pays 11% (pension 9% + health 1% + EOS 1%). Employer pays 18.75% on top.

💰

Income Tax

Progressive brackets 0%→27.5% per Egyptian Tax Law. Taxable income = annual gross minus SI deductions minus personal exemption (EGP 20,000/yr) minus medical premiums.

🔄

Net ↔ Gross

Switch modes to calculate from desired net take-home. Finds the exact gross needed to deliver your target salary using iterative calculation.
