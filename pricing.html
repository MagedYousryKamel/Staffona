FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

+How does Staffona's pricing work?

Payroll management and managed employment are charged as a monthly fee per employee. Recruitment is a one-time placement fee as a percentage of the hired candidate's annual salary. All pricing is transparent ‚Äî no monthly surprises.

+Are prices in USD? Can I pay in other currencies?

Pricing is quoted in USD for international clients. We also invoice in AED, GBP, and EUR. Egyptian clients can be invoiced in EGP at current exchange rates.

+Is there a minimum contract length?

No. Payroll management and managed employment services have no minimum contract term. You can exit with 30 days' notice at any time with no penalties.

+Are there setup or onboarding fees?

There is a one-time onboarding fee of $200 for the first employee, which covers contract preparation, Labor Office registration, and NOSI enrollment. This fee is waived for clients starting with 3 or more employees.

+What's included in the managed employment fee?

The monthly per-employee fee covers: employment contract, Labor Office registration, NOSI enrollment and monthly contributions, income tax withholding and filing, quarterly Form 4 returns, digital payslips, and a dedicated specialist. Employee salary is billed separately at actual cost.

+Can I combine services?

Yes ‚Äî most clients combine services. For example, managed employment plus active recruitment to grow the team, or payroll management plus compliance advisory. Combined packages are quoted together and can reduce the per-service cost.
