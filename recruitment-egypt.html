FAQ

Recruitment in Egypt — Common Questions

+How long does a recruitment search take?

For mid-level professional roles, Staffona typically presents a shortlist of 3 to 5 qualified candidates within 7 to 14 business days. Senior or specialised searches may take 3 to 5 weeks. We never present candidates until confident in their fit.

+What does the 90-day replacement guarantee cover?

If a Staffona-placed candidate leaves or is let go for performance reasons within 90 calendar days of their start date, Staffona conducts a full replacement search at no additional placement fee. This applies to all standard placements.

+Does Staffona recruit for remote roles?

Yes. Many of our clients are GCC, UK, and US companies hiring Egyptian professionals who work fully remotely. Staffona sources and vets candidates and can also provide the managed employment structure to legally employ remote hires in Egypt.

+What industries and roles does Staffona specialise in?

Our strongest track record is in: technology (developers, QA, DevOps, data analysts), finance and accounting, HR and payroll, bilingual support and operations (Arabic/English), and executive search across these functions.

+Is there a retainer or upfront fee?

Standard recruitment charges a placement fee only on successful hire. For executive search or high-volume mandates, a retained arrangement may be proposed. No fee is charged until a candidate is placed and starts. First consultation is always free.
